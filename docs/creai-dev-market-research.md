**Probability**: Medium
**Mitigation**:

**Impact**: High
**Probability**: Medium
**Mitigation**:
- Launch MVP in 3 months, not 6
- Iterate rapidly based on feedback
- Focus on differentiation (multi-agent, no-code)
- Build strong community early

### Risk 3: AutoGPT/n8n License Changes
**Impact**: Medium
**Probability**: Low
**Mitigation**:
- Both are MIT licensed (permissive)
- Fork repositories and maintain own versions
- Build proprietary layer on top
- Diversify underlying tech stack

### Risk 4: Scaling Costs
**Impact**: Medium
**Probability**: Medium
**Mitigation**:
- Usage-based pricing covers costs
- Implement aggressive caching
- Optimize agent execution efficiency
- Start with usage limits per tier

### Risk 5: Regulatory/AI Safety Concerns
**Impact**: Medium
**Probability**: Low
**Mitigation**:
- Implement content filtering
- Human-in-the-loop options
- Audit logs for all agent actions
- Compliance with data protection regulations
- Terms of service prohibiting misuse
